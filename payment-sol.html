Process

How it works

  • Brief interview

    We'll start with a brief interview to understand your business needs and requirements. This will help us identify the best bank, EMI or payment method for your business type and location

  • Offer containing projected costs and terms

    Based on our discussion, we'll provide you with an offer containing projected costs and terms. We'll make sure you have a clear understanding of what you can expect from the payment solution you choose.

  • Collection of documents

    Once you decide to move forward, we'll request the necessary documents from you. This is a crucial step, as the bank or EMI needs to verify your business before opening an account.

  • Application to the payment institution

    With the documents in hand, we'll prepare the case and submit the application to the payment institution. Our team has extensive experience with the application process, so you can rest assured that we'll provide the necessary information and support to get your account opened as quickly as possible.

  • Access to your internet banking

    Once the account is approved, you'll receive your bank or EMI details and access to your internet banking. You'll be ready to start using your account for your business needs.
