Laws and rules

Gambling regulation – laws and rules

Gaming laws and regulations, especially raffle licensing laws, vary in different countries and may be tricky and rapidly changing. While there are jurisdictions where online and offline gambling is completely prohibited, e.g. the UAE, Lebanon, Egypt and Albania, other countries, like the USA, Canada or the Netherlands, have, however, specific local license requirements.
