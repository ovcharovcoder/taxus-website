Contract Review and Drafting Services

Contract Advice and Negotiation Support

At Taxus, we provide contract advice based on sound legal principles and business expertise. We help you understand the terms of a contract, and we’ll even support you during the negotiation discussions if you don’t feel comfortable doing them by yourself.

Contract Review and Update

Our team is qualified and experienced in reviewing contracts to ensure they are safe for you to sign and not opening you up to unknown risks and liabilities. We can also update your existing contracts to bring them in line with new laws or business processes you are implementing.

Contract Drafting

We specialize in drafting business contracts, including website and app terms and conditions, non-disclosure agreements, non-compete agreements, and much more. We tailor your contracts to reflect your business objectives and goals while managing commercial and legal risk.

Contract Management

We provide efficient contract management by maintaining a comprehensive database where all the contracts are uploaded. We monitor these contracts on a regular basis to keep track of important dates, examine them for completeness, identify missing contracts, review them for compliance, and evaluate contract spending.
